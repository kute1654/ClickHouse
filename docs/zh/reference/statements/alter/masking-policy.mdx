修改现有数据脱敏策略。

语法：

```sql
ALTER MASKING POLICY [IF EXISTS] policy_name ON [database.]table
    [UPDATE column1 = expression1 [, column2 = expression2 ...]]
    [WHERE condition]
    [TO {role1 [, role2 ...] | ALL | ALL EXCEPT role1 [, role2 ...]}]
    [PRIORITY priority_number]
```

所有子句均为可选项。只有指定的子句会被更新。
